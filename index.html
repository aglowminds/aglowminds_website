About Us

AGLOW MINDS ACADEMY provides Best classes for UPSC/HAS/HP Allied Services in Baddi, Himachal Pradesh. We are committed to empower young aspirants to explore the best in themselves and the world around them. Our aim at AGLOW MINDS ACADEMY is to help the aspirants become lifelong learners and problem solvers preserving the best in all human achievement and developing a community of highly cultured individuals who seek goodness in all human kind.

Our Vision

We aspire to be an institution of excellence with best use of technology and teaching expertise by creating a nationwide presence for the benefit of students at large.

To be a part of nation-building by nurturing our students, so that they are not just participants in classrooms but active co-creators and empowered agents who can not only pass the toughest exam but also take the immense responsibility of being a Civil Servant.

Our mission is to provide complete civil services solution to budding aspirants. We believe in having a student centric approach with high ethical values and commitment.

Our Ambition: Commit Compete Complete

AGLOW MINDS ACADEMY assures that the students are equipped with the most relevant and comprehensive guidance factoring in all the parameters set by the UPSC for the examination. Since UPSC has been constantly revising and evolving the pattern of Civil Services Examination, We make sure to update our content and modify our pedagogy accordingly.

Therefore, offering to the candidates the relevant study material developed by the most sought after faculty is our foremost objective . The Academy provides complete solution for all 3 stages, i.e., Prelims, Mains as well as interview.
